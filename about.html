ABOUT US

The greatest wealth one can possess in life is definitely good health. When we started our first hospital in 2007, our focus was on delivering quality healthcare to areas that were deprived of basic healthcare services. Today, with operations spread across the Middle East, Europe, UK and India, we still strive hard to deliver the best of care and wellness to people across the World.....

Abreast with medical and technological advancements, VPS Lakeshore Hospital is committed to compassionate patient care and caters to people from all over the globe. With top-of-the-line healthcare professionals from across the world, VPS Lakeshore Hospital will continue to be the best in the country delivering premium-quality healthcare services to the people of Kerala. The hospital stands tall with 600 beds, more than 40 specialities and a centre of excellence for multi-organ transplant services, and is already one of the largest quaternary care hospitals in India with state-of-the-art facilities and an increasing number of successful procedures. We are glad that we have VPS Lakeshore Hospital as part of our global healthcare mission and together we look forward to providing quality healthcare services and shaping a healthier future.
